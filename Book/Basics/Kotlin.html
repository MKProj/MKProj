KOTLIN BASICS

Kotlin is a very common language to see in the world of Android Development, and with it's modern approach to Java, it is quite a language to look at. Not only is it made for developers, but is created by the same company that makes IDES such as IntelliJ, PyCharm, CLion; Jetbrains!!! Learn the basics of Kotlin to understand the fundamentals so when you decide to make Android applications, you can understand the general idea of the program.
