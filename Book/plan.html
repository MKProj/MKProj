MKProject Book Plans

The MKProject-Books project has a goal of providing books with a different way to approach a topic, such as Basics series with key concepts, or More series going into more detail of important sections. This has been a trial and error project considering the amount of revisions done to it, whether starting from studying references (MKRL) to various studying formats (Tomorrow's Study) to now various book series. I hope you enjoy, and consider contributing to make this dream a reality!
